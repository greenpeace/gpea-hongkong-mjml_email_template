People Power to combat Air Pollution üë™In July, more than 40 citizen scientists joined us to install 115 diffusion tubes from lamp posts to homes in different districts. The samples collected are currently being tested for nitrogen dioxide levels to establish the quality of our air in Hong Kong. Please stay tuned for the report to be released early Q4.

The Task Force, which commenced in June, received over 120 submissions.
