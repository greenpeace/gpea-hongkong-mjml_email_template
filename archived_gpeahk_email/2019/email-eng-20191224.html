More from Hong Kong
 
 
Climate action and people power more to come! On November 29th, Hong Kong joined global climate strikes and march to bring attention to the climate emergency. Greenpeace also supported the rally organized by School strike 4 climate, Extinction Rebellion HK, 350.org and Waste-Free HK. We are urging local authorities to take immediate action to increase renewable energy, our city only has a 0.1% renewable energy share in the market, which is trailing behind other Asian cities.

#PlasticFreeNow 3 years old and growing strong In 2019, with over 20K supporters, including you, we have archived some milestones in moving major supermarket brands to reduce plastic packaging: Greenpeace held the first supermarket multi-stakeholder forum in Asia and witnessed a citywide supermarket first to practice the "package-less sales model". From Our Offices Worldwide
 
 
A landmark victory for climate justice! Big polluters could be found legally and morally liable for #humanrights harms to Filipinos resulting from #climatechange, the Commission on Human Rights of the Philippines (CHR) announced the result of a years-long investigation into corporate responsibility for climate change on Dec 9th. Greenpeace is among the signatories to a petition on December 2015 to CHR to ask for the investigation.
